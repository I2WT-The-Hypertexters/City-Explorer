Places to visit:



Banff National Park – Alberta

Banff is renowned for the sweetness of the Canadian Rockies , the luxurious landscapes, and unique thermal hot springs. With over one thousand miles of hike able trails within the summer, pristine lakes to canoe or kayak, and superb skiing within the winter, Banff is actually an outside enthusiast’s paradise. The downtown area offers quaint shops and delightful hotels.

Polar bear safari

It is rarely that you simply are ready to come face-to-face with a wild polar bear , witness an white fox across the tundra or the aurora borealis dance across the sky. But Churchill in Canada, which is considered the ice bear capital of the planet , is one place that you simply can do all three. The isolated town is found within the province of Manitoba, in arctic Canada, and it's this remoteness that provides it its charm.

Canadian canoeing

Canadian canoeing may be a must-do activity during your visit to the good White North. The country boasts countless stunning lakes and rivers to explore. The Canadian Canoe has many routes and trip reports from across Canada. For instance , if you're going for a holiday in Toronto then you'll be given alot of choices. Ontario is understood for its plentiful waterways, with routes that are perfect to start canoeing on.

See the Northern Lights

The Aurora covers much of Canada and a few of the simplest places to ascertain the northern lights are the provinces of British Columbia , Yukon, Northern Saskatchewan, Newfoundland, Northwest Territories and Nova Scotia. A map by Canadian Geographic shows exactly where the simplest places are in Canada to view the best colours of the aurora borealis . Remember that a number of the more remote northern provinces experience the atmospheric phenomenon in summer, so if you would like to ascertain the Lights head over in winter when you’re guaranteed clear, dark skies.
