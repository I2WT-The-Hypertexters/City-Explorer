HTML and CSS of the website

I am a developer of this website and in collaboration with other three of the HyperTexters, we created a website of our favourite places we have visited or lived.

Everyone has worked on a country in the best way possible, and mine is Italy, an interesting country with great history and fabulous sightseeing places which left me speechless.

All the HTML and CSS used on my page and the others' are systematized mostly in div's so no mistakes are made. That is why you will see mostly div's with different classes and styles such as the text align and the display so the pages look like tables, something really good in my opinion.

It was a challenging experience but well worthy, because my work and all the time i spent will defenitely help other people that are searching for beautiful destinations which will not disappoint them.
