The Photographer

The Photographer strives to take an even better picture than the last. Their main objective is to find and document the most beautiful places on earth. While historic locations can be interesting, they are unlikely to be attractive to a Photographer unless it is equally photogenic.

Places to visit

White Sands National Monument, NM

A sterling locale for any aspiring Photographer is undoubtly the White Sands National Monument. The unique landscape mimics snow without the freezing temperatures. Besides being a great location for photography based on novelty alone, it also features numerous trails and mountains to hike for entirely new perspectives. The wildlife at the White Sands National Monument is truly stunning, this unusually harsh enviornment creating unique adapations of the species living here. Mockingbirds, Ravens, Foxes, and albino varations of them all prowl the wastes.

Kauai, HI

While the beautiful beaches of Hawaii may be a popular destination for taking photographs, it certainly earns its spot on this list. Just a few activities that are worthy of documentation include surfing, snorkeling, scuba diving, helicopter touring, houseback riding, ziplining, loauing, and ofcourse hiking. This island has a little bit of everything so regardless of your interests, there is an attraction or photo opportunity for you!

Seattle, WA

Seattle has to be the most varied location on this list! It has coastal spots, dewy landscapes, and breathtaking architecture woven together into a bustling metropolis. The iconic Space Needle is an observation tower found in this spot. Seattle also has a rich culture and deep roots in political demonstrations. If you are interested in seeing rare species in their natural enviornment Seattle is a great place for whale watching as well!
